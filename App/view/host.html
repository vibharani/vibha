Education Services

Administrator Training

  • Administrators

Our approach to training begins with a fundamental understanding that your training needs will evolve. As a new administrator or power user just starting your implementation, you need a foundation of product knowledge to make informed configuration and design decisions with your Implementation Consultant for a business requirements meeting. As a more experienced administrator or power user, you need a deeper product understanding of functionality and best practices.

We have training programs, offerings, and curriculum that align to your maturity with the product and enable you to stay on top of each new feature release. As a first step, navigate to the  Training Calendar to register for live, virtual instructor-led training classes that we offer on a regular basis. This class format is the most effective way to learn the application as our experienced instructors guide you on the best way to use the product, facilitate classroom discussions with your peers and give you hands-on practice with the product in a controlled setting. They are part of your Premium Training Subscription or available for purchase a la carte.

Should staffing changes occur and new administrators need to be trained on your existing configuration, we offer the  New Administrator Acceleration Program, a combination of Host Analytics educational and consulting services. The new administrator is trained on core Host Analytics functionality as well as on your own organization’s specific configuration and processes. Host Analytics consultants review client-specific business processes and the current application configuration. Our consultants also provide best-practices insights to maximize the value of the application.

Training is not just a one-time event. It’s an ongoing process to increase adoption and productivity – the keys to maximizing your return on investment.
